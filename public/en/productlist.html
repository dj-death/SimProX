  • MarkSimos

    Strategy, Marketing & General Management (FMCG)

    MarkSimos is a Business Simulation Game that allows you to run a company in a realistic, yet risk-free virtual environment within an intensive time frame. The game simulates a dynamic market in FMCG Industry. Through sharing and experiential learning, participants are encouraged to create a CEO perspective and to get a well-rounded view of the firm and its functions.

    LEARN MORE >

  • CasaSimos

    Strategy, Marketing & General Management (Durable goods)

    CasaSimos simulates durable goods industry in various markets. Participants learn to apply a range of business concepts from pricing, brand management and marketing to strategy through the simulation. In addition, participants train themselves to react to competition in a risk-free environment

    LEARN MORE >

  • eTales

    Strategy & Cross-Channel Management

    eTales is the first business simulation in the world that focuses on the manufacturer / retailer relationship in the clicks-and-mortar environment. The simulation integrates the effects of internet-enabled shopping and consumer empowerment through social media on the supplier and retailer relationship.

    LEARN MORE >

  • ChangeMan

    Leadership & Change Management

    ChangeMan is a concept and a set of technologies designed to support managers in managing complex changes in organizations. tools will help the managers map out their changes an make them understand their own role better. At the core of ChangeMan lies HCD’s simulation technologies. These technologies are designed to reach a high level of learning impact within a short time frame.

    LEARN MORE >

  • LeaderStyle

    Leadership & Change Management

    LeaderStyle is a comprehensive tool that helps you discover your own leadership style. Based on established theories, LeaderStyle defines a broad spectrum of leadership styles and is an efficient tool to identify your strengths and weaknesses as a leader.
