Join Us

At HCD Learning, experiential learning: endless potential is not just a slogan it’s a way of living. Team members thrive in a dynamic environment laced with opportunity to expand knowledge and gain experience by participating in ‘hands on’ projects.

Web Front-end developer

HCD Learning is looking for a front-end web application developer experienced in building interfaces to rich Internet applications to join our development team. We are looking for an individual skilled in highly dynamic web interface development (HTML, JavaScript, AJAX, jQuery), who is passionate not only in front-end development AngularJS/WebSocket, but also Backend development with NodeJS/MongoDB.

We use a variety of technologies in our platform, such as AngularJS, NodeJS, MongoDB, Socket.io, Lazarus, Delphi and more! You don’t need to come in knowing all of these; we want talented engineers who can pick up the tools they need on the fly and who share our passion for writing coding that will help our clients to get transformational experiential learning experience. We are very welcome to people who commit themselves to be a full-stack programmer in the future.

Here you can find competitive salary and benefits package, together with working environments that are modern, well-equipped and comfortable. For developer, we especially offer MacBook + 24 inch external monitor as your equipment, you are also welcome to bring your own computer to our working place. As developer,It is also possible for you to work from home if your self-manegament ability has been verified by our teams.

HCD Learning has a cross-cultural team working environment, our members comes from Korea, India, Poland andChina. Here you will find chance to work with talented people from different country and cultural.

Essential Job functions:

  • - Builds software applications – Follows coding standards, builds appropriate unit tests, integration tests and deployment scripts
  • - Assists in defining software architectures – Collaborates with leads to explore existing systems, determines areas of complexity, potential risks to successful implementation, learns the applications capabilities
  • - Communicates continually with the client and project teams – Explains progress on the development effort
  • - Owns success – Takes responsibility for successful delivery of the solutions
  • - Translates designs and style guides provided by the UI/UX team into functional user interfaces, ensuring cross browser compatibility and performance
  • - Contributes to continual improvement by suggesting improvements to user interface, software architecture or new technologies

Required Skills:

  • Required Skills:
  • - Basic knowledge with HTTP protocol, Web socket
  • - Be familiar with Git and *nix as main development platform

Bonus Points:

  • - Experience in Object-Oriented Pascal(Delphi/Lazarus) would be a plus
  • - Experience in Socket.io/Nodejs/MongoDB/Mongoose would be a plus
